Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula, etc.)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled (optional)

For the Dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

For the Grilled Chicken:

  1. Preheat your grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  1. Brush the chicken breasts with olive oil on both sides.
  1. Season the chicken with salt, black pepper, dried oregano, and garlic powder.
  1. Grill the chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165¬∞F (75¬∞C) and the chicken is cooked through.
  1. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Then, slice it into thin strips.

For the Salad: 6. In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, and red onion.

  1. If desired, add crumbled feta cheese on top.

For the Dressing: 8. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, salt, and black pepper until well combined.

To Assemble: 9. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat.

  1. Top the salad with the grilled chicken strips.
  1. Serve immediately and enjoy your healthy Grilled Chicken and Vegetable Salad!
